Work

    Corporate identity and PC application design for a neurotech startup LOOXID Labs.

  • LINE Kids' Phone

    A kids-friendly premium smart watch with new user experience.

  • MEMO

    A clinically-tested wearable device - designed to monitor important vital signs (e.g. blood pressure) right on the wrist.

  • Talk2Me

    An interactive mobile language-learning service for people who want casual yet instructive experience.

  • GOPAX press kit

    A printed press kit including brochure and presentation folder for cryptocurrency exchange GOPAX.

  • AMe concept video

    A concept explainer video for wearable 360° camera AMe (now FITT360).
