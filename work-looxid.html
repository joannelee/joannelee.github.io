LOOXID Labs

LOOXID Labs is a neurotech startup developing eye-brain interface platform LX-I, which combines the latest eye-tracking technology and brain signal analysis into a single headset.


In this project, corporate identity for LOOXID Labs and PC application interface of LUCY which connects the headset to user's computer were designed.

  • ClientLOOXID Labs
  • Period 2015
  • Scope CI, PC App UX/GUI

CI

Visual identity of LOOXID Labs is developed to symbolise the concept of convergence, and to represent both cutting edge technology and energetic spirit of LOOXID Labs.

PC App UX/GUI

LUCY, a Window PC application, connects hardware device LX-I to users. LUCY is designed in a form of widgets so that users can turn raw data graphs on and off. Keeping consistent corporate identity and considering general users, LUCY is designed to have easy and friendly look & feel with bright colours and round-shaped elements.
