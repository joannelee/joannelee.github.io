Product concept

What differentiates AMe from the other 360° cameras is that you can take 360° video from an actual first-person point of view as it is wearable. The designer's job here was to come up with ideas to further this feature into detailed user scenarios, and the following were chosen as the product concept keywords: remind, share, and experience.

{% include project-image.html filename="concepts" alt="The product concept keywords of AMe" title="The product concept keywords of AMe" %}

1) Remind: Play the videos taken with AMe on a VR headset to remind and relive unforgettable moments.
2) Share: Use the AMe mobile application to make your own AMe video album and share it on social media.
3) Experience: Play other users' videos on a VR headset to 'experience' what they did and how they felt.

Artworks
