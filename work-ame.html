AMe concept video

AMe(now FITT360) is a 360° neckband wearable camera made by Linkflow Inc., a spin-off company from Samsung Electronics C-Lab.


A concept explainer video of AMe was the final output of this project. Making UX features, making video storyboard and artworks, directing video production were all included in the project scope. This video was played at Samsung Developer Conference 2016.

  • ClientLinkflow Inc.
  • Period 2016
  • Scope UX feature making, video directing

UX features

The main product feature of AMe is that you can take 360° video in true first-person-view because it's a wearable 360° camera. So the question started from finding meanings of these videos in UX point of view, and ended up with three main features - remind, share, and experience.

1) Remind: Play videos taken with AMe on VR headset so you can remind and relive specific moments.
2) Share: Use AMe mobile application to make your own AMe video album and share it on social media.
3) Experience: Play other users' videos on VR headset so you can 'experience' what they did and how they felt.

Artworks

To put emphasis on parts that were taken with AMe, all artworks were designed to be less like actual images, but more like hand-drawn pictures with crayons on coloured papers.
