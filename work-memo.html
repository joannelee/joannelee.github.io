MEMO

MEMO is a clinically-tested wearable blood pressure monitor. It is designed to continuously and accurately monitor important vital signs, right on the wrist.


UX of both the wearable device and associated mobile application were designed alongside product design team. Designing BI and GUI was also included in the scope of this project.

  • ClientHUINNO, Inc.
  • Period 2015
  • Scope Device UX/GUI, BI, iPhone App UX/GUI

Device UX/GUI

MEMO innovatively locates a blood pressure monitor on the wrist. The special purpose of this device required careful considerations in terms of ergonomics (e.g. placement and shape of a sensor and buttons) for easy operation and accurate measurement of vital signs.

These are the three major considerations for UI of MEMO; easy and quick initial setup, minimal steps to access blood pressure measuring function, and straightforward feedback during measuring. GUI of MEMO focused on presenting visually appealing graphics on a display with low resolution and legibility.

BI

The overall structure and shape of MEMO logotype shares the same ‘stable and bold’ characteristics with HUINNO logotype. Deep bluish black and gold were chosen as two main colours to enhance a rich image of MEMO brand by combining them.

iPhone App UX/GUI

MEMO iPhone app is designed as a platform where users can intuitively understand the changes of blood pressure. Users can also manually input their own health data and manage them. Signing up and device pairing are simplified and designed as the easiest process.
